ABOUT THIS STORY

DOUG is a short story about an ordinary guy who, through no fault of his own, ends up in the depths of Hell Inc. — a bureaucratic nightmare where even the damned have to deal with paperwork, HR departments, and middle management demons.

This story explores what happens when the afterlife runs like a corporation, complete with employee handbooks, cybernetic implants, and an unholy union of the TSA and the DMV.
